A "What to Expect?" For Birth Doulas in NYC

Brief: A full stack web application for NY Metro Doula Facebook group birth doulas to inform one another about what is available at certain NYC Hospitals.

Concept: While doulas often work on their own and social media provides a place to share information, there isn't a specific designated place to do so. This web application provides an easy and streamlined process to keep each other "in the know."

Role: UX/UI, web development and design
